About Us

Our purpose is to affordably and reliably service the vehicles you’ve come to depend on.
Established in May of 2019, SRS Tire and Auto in Boone NC is a family owned business. Its daily operations and customer service are carried out by trusted members of management. Customer vehicles are serviced by our reliable ASE technicians that have over 30 years of combined experience. We pride ourselves on absolute honesty!
Any one of us, if asked will suggest your best course of action based on facts.

Our Services

At SRS Tire and Auto in Boone NC, we offer a complete line of automotive repair services and vehicle accessories to fit your needs. We service and repair Domestic, European, Hybrid, and Imports!
You can trust SRS Tire and Auto’s ASE Certified Technicians to provide you with quality service at a reasonable price. We are a NAPA Pro Care Center, which allows SRS Tire and Auto to provide you the best in parts and warranties. NAPA Pro Care warranties are 24 months/24,000 miles nation wide coverage on our repairs.
